Contracting States shall exchange such

the domestic laws of the Contracting
States concerning taxes covered by the
Agreement insofar as the taxation there-

secret in the same manner as information

State and shall be disclosed only to per-

ment or prosecution in respect of, or the
determination of appeals in relation to,
the taxes covered by the Agreement.
