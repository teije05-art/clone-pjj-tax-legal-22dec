No. 23/2015/TT-BKHCN Ha Noi, November 13th, 2015

CIRCULAR
REGULATING THE IMPORT OF MACHINERY, EQUIPMENT AND
TECHNOLOGICAL LINE ALREADY USED

Pursuant to the Government's Decree No 20/2013/ND-CP dated February 26th, 2013
defining the functions, tasks, power and organizational structure of the Ministry of
Science and Technology;

Pursuant to the Government's Decree No. 187/2013/ND-CP dated November 20th, 2013
detailing the implementation of the Commercial Law on activities of international
purchase and sale of goods and activities of agency for sale, purchase, processing and
transit of goods involving foreign parties;

At the proposals of the Director General of the Department of Technology Appraisal,
Examination and Assessment, the Director General of the Legal Affairs Department;

The Minister of Science and Technology promulgates the Circular regulating the import
of machinery, equipment and technological line already used.

Chapter I
GENERAL PROVISIONS
Article 1. Scope of regulation

1. This Circular deals with criteria, dossier, procedures for the import of machinery,
equipment, technological line already used, including components, accessories, substitute
parts already used with HS codes included in Chapter 84 and Chapter 85 prescribed in the
List of Vietnam's exports, imports promulgated by the Ministry of Finance in Circular
No. 103/2015/TT-BTC dated July Olst, 2015, that are used for the production and
business activities in the country, do not belong to the List of goods banned from import
prescribed by the Government, Prime Minister, Ministries and Ministerial agencies.

2. This Circular does not apply to the import of machinery, equipment and technological
line already used under the following forms:

a) Transit; border gate transfer;

Article 2, Subjects of application

This Circular applies to:

1. Organizations, individuals (hereinafter referred to as enterprises) importing machinery,
equipment, technological line already used; importing components, accessories,

substitute parts already used.

2. Assessing organizations that conduct the assessment of machinery, equipment,
technological line already used as prescribed in this Circular.

3. State managing agencies involved in the import of machinery, equipment and
technological line already used as prescribed in Article 1 of this Circular.

Article 3. Interpretation of terms
In this Circular, the terms below are construed as follows:

1. Machinery, equipment is the completed structure including details, groups of details or
parts linked each other for the operation, movement under the designed intended use.

2. Technological line is a system of equipment, tools, facilities arranged, installed
according to diagram, technological process to ensure synchronous operation for
production.

3. Components, accessories, substitute parts are details, groups of details composing
machinery, equipment to be imported for the purpose of replacement, repairing in order
to ensure or improve efficiency, operation capacity of operating machinery, equipment,
technological line.

4. Components, accessories, substitute parts already used are components, accessories,
substitute parts which are detached from used machinery, equipment and technological
line.

5. Equipment age (counted by year) is the duration determined from manufacturing year
to importing year of machinery, equipment and technological line already used. The
importing year is the year the goods arrived at Vietnam’s ports.

6. Machinery, equipment and technological line already used (hereinafter referred to as
used equipment) are machinery, equipment and technological line that have been already
installed and operated after ex-workshop.

Article 4. Principles of managing the import

1. Used equipment to be imported must comply with the provisions of the Government,
the Prime Minister and Ministries, Ministerial agencies on importing goods.

2. With regard to imported used equipment that are regulated in documents of Ministries,
Ministerial agencies, it shall comply with the provisions of such Ministries, Ministerial
agencies; the remaining cases shall comply with the provisions of this Circular.

3. It is not allowed to import used equipment that are proclaimed the removal due to be
out of date, bad quality and causing environmental pollution proclaimed by the Ministry
of Science and Technology on its Portal.
4. Enterprises are encouraged to carry the assessment of used equipment from exporting
countries. If the goods are assessed upon the arrival at Vietnam's ports, importing
enterprises may apply the regime on putting the goods in storage prescribed in this
Circular.
Chapter II

REQUIREMENTS FOR IMPORT OF USED EQUIPMENT

Article 5. General requirement

Used equipment to be imported must meet the requirements on safety, energy saving and
environmental protection under the provisions of current laws.

Article 6. Specific requirements
1. Used equipment shall be imported if it meets the following criteria:
a) Equipment age must not exceed 10 years;

b) It is produced under standards that are appropriate to the National Technical
Regulations or National Standards of Vietnam or appropriate to the G7 Nations'
Standards on safety, energy - saving and environmental protection.

2. With regard to used equipment of investment projects, including projects of new
investment and projects of expansion investment falling into the following cases:

a) Projects subject to the decision on investment policies;

b) Projects that are subject to the carrying out of procedures for issuance of investment
registration Certificate but not subject to the decision on investment policies.

The provisions in Clause | of this Article shall not be applied if there is the list of used
equipment in the dossier of investment project, the investment policies have been decided
by authorities, the investment registration Certificate have been issued by investment
registering agencies.

In necessary case, agencies competent to decide investment policies, investment
registering agencies may consult technological assessment opinions of agencies
specialized in science and technology about used equipment in the dossier of project
before making decision on investment policies or issuing the investment registration
Certificate.

3. With regard to components, accessories, substitute parts already used: They are only
imported when producing enterprises have the demand of repairing, replacement for the
operating equipment at the enterprises. Producing enterprises may import themselves or
authorize the import to other enterprises.

4. In necessary cases, depend on characters of each business line, sector assigned,
Ministers, Head of Ministerial agencies shall promulgate the regulations on requirements
of equipment age lower than that specified in Point a Clause | Article 6 of this Circular
for used equipment (lower than 10 years ) and notify to the Ministry of Science and
Technology for uniform management.

Article 7. The import dossier of used equipment

The import dossier of used equipment: apart from the import dossier under the Customs
Law, enterprises shall supplement | set of documents, including:

1. With regard to used equipment of investment projects specified in Clause 2 Article 6 of
this Circular:

- 01 authenticated copy of the Decision on investment policies or the investment
registration Certificate enclosed with 01 original of the List of used equipment to be
imported in the project dossier.

2. With regard to other cases of importing used equipment (including investment projects
approved under the Decision on investment policies or the investment registration
certificate according to the provisions of the Investment Law without the List of used
equipment to be imported): Technical document stating manufacturing year and
manufacturing standards of the used equipment, including one of the following
documents:

a) The producer's manufacturing year and standard Certificate of used equipment in
accordance with Clause | Article 6 of this Circular ;

b) 01 original of the assessment Certificate of an assessing organization specified in
Clause 2 Article 10 of this Circular regarding manufacturing year and manufacturing
standards of used equipment in accordance with Clause 1 Article 6 of this Circular .

Article 8. Procedures for import of used equipment

1. Based on the documents submitted by enterprises, customs office shall determine if
used equipment meets the requirements specified in Article 6 of this Circular, then it shall
carry out the procedures for customs clearance as prescribed.

2. Putting goods into storage:

a) If at the time of importing used equipment, the dossier specified in Article 7 of this
Circular is not adequate, enterprises may put goods in storage after submitting to customs
offices the following documents:

- 0L original written assessment Registration certified by assessing organization
according to Clause 2 Article 10 of this Circular;

- 01 original written request for putting goods in storage according to Form No.
09/BQHH/GSQL Annex V of Circular No. 38/2015/TT-BTC dated March 25th, 2015 of
the Ministry of Finance defining customs procedures; customs inspection and
supervision; import and export duties and tax administration towards imports, exports.

1. To assume the prime responsibility for, and coordinate with Ministries, Ministerial-

level agencies in the activity of managing the import of used equipment specified in this
Circular and in relevant documents.

2. To publicize periodically on the Ministry of Science and Technology’s Portal the List
of used equipment that is proclaimed the abolishment by other countries due to be out of
date, bad quality and causing environmental pollution.

3. To coordinate with Ministries, Ministerial-level agencies, People's Committee of
provinces and centrally - affiliated cities in organizing the inspection of the compliance
with laws on safety, economical and efficient use of energy, environmental protection of
used equipment which is imported by enterprises when it is put into operation and
handling violations under current provisions.

4. To coordinate with the General Department of Vietnam Customs in making statistics
of import of machinery, equipment, technological line already used (name of importing
enterprise, address, field using machinery and equipment, manufacturing country, import
value), cases of violation that must be re-exported and/or handled shall be reported to the
Prime Minister.

5. To summarize and announce the List of Vietnamese and foreign assessing
organizations that meet the provisions in Clause 2 Article 10 of this Circular (name,
address, website, telephone, fax, assessment registration form, assessment certificate
form) on the Ministry of Science and Technology's Portal.

6. The Ministry of Science and Technology shall assign Department of Technology
Appraisal, Examination and Assessment to implement this Circular.

Article 12. Responsibilities of enterprises

1. To strictly perform the import of used equipment according to the provisions provided
in this Circular and related legal normative documents.

2. Enterprise importing used equipment for commercial purpose (the equipment is not
used directly by the importing enterprise itself) is only allowed to sell the goods after
completing the customs clearance procedures according to the provisions provided in
Article 8 of this Circular.

3. To be subject to the inspection of state managing agencies regarding safety, energy -
saving and environmental protection after the customs clearance of equipment, during
installment and operation of production and business deployment as well as subject to
sanction if committing violations against the provisions of this Circular and related legal
normative documents.

Article 13. Special cases

When equipment age of used equipment is over 10 years but the enterprise needs to
import the equipment for maintaining the production and business activities, based
dossier and proposals of the enterprise, the Ministry of Science and Technology shall
coordinate with other Ministries, branches in considering, making decision.

Article 14. Dealing with breaches

1. If after reaching Vietnam's port and border gate, the used equipment which has been
assessed fails to meet the requirements provided in Clause | Article 6 of this Circular, the
enterprise shall be subject to the sanction measures under current laws. In the course of
inspection, violation handling, the authority shall decide on permitting the re-assessment
upon the request of the enterprise.

2. In case used equipment is granted the customs clearance and put into installment and
use, if during the operation, the used equipment fails to meet the requirements on safety,
energy-saving and environmental protection, the enterprise shall be subject to the
sanction measures under current laws.

Article 15. Transitional provisions

Sale and purchase contracts that have been signed and imported used equipment that has
been loaded up means of transport before the effective date of this Circular shall not be
subject to the regulations of this Circular.

Article 16. Effect
1. This Circular takes effect from July 01st, 2016.

2. As from the effective date of this Circular, the following documents are expired:
Circular No. 20/2014/TT-BKHCN dated July 15th, 2014 of the Ministry of Science and
Technology prescribing the import of used machinery, equipment, technological line;
Announcement No. 2527/TB-BKHCN dated September 06th, 2012 of the Ministry of
Science and Technology on suspending the import of used machinery, equipment,
technological line.

3. When the legislative documents referred to in this Circular are amended or replaced,
the new documents shall be applied.

4. If there are any problems arising in the course of implementation, Ministries,
Ministerial agencies, Government - affiliated agencies, organizations, individuals should
reflect to the Ministry of Science and Technology for amending, supplementing to this
Circular for being appropriate.
