CIT fieldwork procedures (to read simultaneously with the existing CIT instruction) – documents to be vouched and obtained for WP purposes
First and foremost – understand your client’s business 
Vietnamese Accounting System (VAS)
Please always check if the client comply with VAS – basically we will focus on (i) chart of account; (ii) language of account/supporting documents, etc; (iii) accounting software; (iv)Printing out all accounting voucher for signature; (v) filing system; 
Method – we should always remember when we vouch; we can interview the client as well
B1-Sales
EPE – to obtain 1 sample sales contract; review if this is overseas sales + processing contract – if to non EPE – red flag; to check sample invoice – in accordance to Circular 153; to check customs clearances form – on site import/export?; timing of issuing invoice + sale recognition; is there any delivery note to prove to goods received; 
Non-EPE –  continue from the above; to reconcile the VAT returns to total turnover – reasonableness test;

B1-Sales return
To check if the contract term mentioned this 
To check if there is minute or invoice is issued in accordance with Circular 153
For goods return – please check the customs clearance if the goods actual returned. 

B4.1– Depreciation 
To check – registration with tax department; 
To obtain fixed asset register tally to the audited financial statement 
To check useful life in accordance to Circular 203
To check the start date of depreciation – invoice date and date put in used – normally different in first month put to use;
From the fixed asset register, sampling check the documentation under Circular 203 – i.e. contract (from overseas – please check the incoterm – FCT purposes); invoice; payment evidence; customs clearance form for imported FA; 
To check list of long term prepayment  i.e. FA below 10 million;
To check land use right amortization 
To check intangible FA
To check CIP – similar to FA – please obtain the list of CIP addition during the year – from the list – please check the contract, invoice, customs clearance form, payment evidence, etc. This category normally involve FCT implications


B4.2 - Interest
To check any long term loan – registration with the SBV;
To check any loan from non-financial institution – interest rate ok?
To check if the company fully contributed the charter capital – No – red flag 

B4.3 – Expenses without legitimate invoice + B4.5-Non business expenses
Method: Obtain PL => TB => GL => supporting documents. From the GL, please review (i) item material in nature; (ii) item material in amount – from the GL, select the sample then vouch to the supporting documents. PLS MARK THE GL FOR THE SAMPLE SELECTED AND THE POPULATION NEEDED TO BE REVIEWED BY MIC.
For manufacturing company – please check if they register the material consumption lever – COGS – please also check randomly
Please pay special attention to tax sensitive account e.g. entertainment, travelling, miscellaneous, staff welfare; donation;  medical expenses for expatriate; expenses for hostel; golf;  Normally these accounts consist of a lot of hidden cost – we should pay more attention to this. 
Please pay attention to overseas invoices – criteria must be met; - red flag 
For payment without invoice – please consider PIT withholding issue 10%/20%. 
(CONTINUE OTHER STEPS NORMALLY PERFORM)

B4.6 – Employment related costs
Method: Obtain PL – list of payroll => check with labour contract, internal labour agreement => check to PIT declaration – all these 3 documents shall be tally. 
Check if any bonus (Tet, occasional); 
Check if any payment to seasonal worker without labour contract – Form 23 has been obtained? 
Please check from GL any payment to employee not being declared – e.g. payment of house, utility, water for expatiate; from travelling account – if there any home passage not declare, etc; 
Please check 1 local PIT/SHUI and 1 expat PIT  calculation; 

B4.7 – Accrual 
Method: Obtain the breakdown tally to audited FS. WP must show the breakdown; 
Perform the normal procedure;

B4.10 – A&P
For non-manufacturing company – please pay specially attention on this. Normally may exceed cap. 

PLEASE ALSO REVIEW PRIOR YEARS TAX ADJUSTMENTS OR ANY TAX MINUTES TO ENSURE CONSISTENCY

Other income 
Method: Obtain the breakdown tally to audited FS. WP must show the breakdown; 
Determination of other income – follow Circular 18


CIT incentive 
Please determine the company’s CIT incentive status 
Please pay attention any risk of expansion if the company changes it IC – red flag
EPE – be aware of 4+7 – red flag

Tax losses c/f
Please determine the tax losses c/f status 
If there any tax planning opportunity
Can other income offset again current year tax losses? 

Other items, PIT and FCT issues – as normal
